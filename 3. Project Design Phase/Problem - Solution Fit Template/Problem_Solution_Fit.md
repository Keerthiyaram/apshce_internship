# Problem - Solution Fit

This matrix maps the core user problems identified during the Empathy Phase to the specific features designed to solve them, validating the product's market fit.

| Identified User Problem | Psychological Impact | Proposed Feature / Solution | Success Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data Entry Friction**: Searching for every ingredient takes too long. | Frustration, high abandonment rate. | **Quick-Log & Saved Meals**: Save complex meals as a single entity; log macro totals directly. | 80% of active users utilize saved meals within their first 14 days. |
| **Macro Math Confusion**: Hitting precise daily targets feels like solving a puzzle. | Decision fatigue, feeling overwhelmed. | **Smart Recipe Recommendations**: Engine suggests meals that perfectly fill the remaining daily macros. | Users who use recommendations hit their macro targets 30% more often than those who don't. |
| **Lack of Context**: Knowing *what* to eat, but not *why*. | Short-term compliance without long-term habit change. | **Contextual Education & Guides**: In-app articles explaining metabolic adaptation, fiber benefits, etc. | 50% of users read at least one guide per week; correlation with higher retention. |
| **Accountability & Trust**: Generic advice doesn't apply to specific medical or physical needs. | Feeling isolated; plateauing progress. | **Dietitian Consultation Portal**: Direct messaging and video consults with certified professionals. | 15% conversion rate of free users to premium consultation users after hitting a plateau. |
| **Dietary Restrictions Isolation**: Most apps assume a standard omnivorous diet. | Alienation; constant manual filtering. | **Global Dietary Toggles**: Setting account to "Vegan" automatically filters all recipe suggestions and default databases. | High satisfaction score (CSAT > 4.5/5) among users with specific dietary restrictions. |
