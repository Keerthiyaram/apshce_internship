# Customer Journey Map

## Persona: Alex (Health-Conscious Professional)
**Goal:** Lose 10 lbs sustainably while working a busy corporate job.

---

## 1. Discovery & Awareness
**Action:** Alex feels sluggish and realizes their clothes are getting tight. They search "easy macro tracker for busy people" on Google or see a targeted ad on Instagram.
**Touchpoints:** Social Media Ads, App Store Listing, Fitness Blogs.
**Emotions:** Motivated but skeptical. Worried about complicated apps.
**App Role:** Provide clear messaging: "Track macros in 2 minutes a day. No math required."

## 2. Onboarding (Day 1)
**Action:** Alex downloads the app and signs up.
**Touchpoints:** `Register.js` form, Welcome Screens.
**Experience:** Alex inputs age, weight, height, and goal. The app instantly calculates a daily calorie and macro target. Alex selects "No Dairy" in dietary restrictions.
**Emotions:** Excited. The onboarding feels personal and low-friction.
**App Role:** Calculate TDEE accurately. Set up the local environment and profile state seamlessly.

## 3. First Use & Daily Habit (Days 2-14)
**Action:** Logging meals daily.
**Touchpoints:** `Dashboard`, `Meals` view.
**Experience:** Alex logs breakfast (oatmeal) and sees the macro ring fill up. At lunch, Alex uses the "Quick Log" feature for a salad. At dinner, Alex checks the app, sees they need 40g of protein, and uses the "Suggest Recipe" feature.
**Emotions:** Satisfied, empowered. The visual rings provide a gamified sense of accomplishment.
**App Role:** Ensure lightning-fast UI response. Keep the recipe suggestions relevant and strictly dairy-free as requested during onboarding.

## 4. Hitting a Plateau (Day 21)
**Action:** Alex notices weight loss has stalled for a week.
**Touchpoints:** `Education` (Guides), `Consultations` (Consult).
**Experience:** Alex feels frustrated. They browse the "Guides" section and read an article about "Metabolic Adaptation". Seeking personalized advice, Alex books a 15-minute chat with a dietitian via the "Consult" tab.
**Emotions:** Anxious turning to reassured.
**App Role:** Provide easy access to educational resources and professional help to prevent churn.

## 5. Retention & Advocacy (Day 60+)
**Action:** Alex hits the 10 lb weight loss goal.
**Touchpoints:** `Profile` (Progress tracking), Social Sharing (Future feature).
**Experience:** Alex updates their goal from "Lose Weight" to "Maintain". They recommend the app to a co-worker.
**Emotions:** Proud, loyal to the brand.
**App Role:** Smoothly transition the user's macro goals without requiring a full account reset. Celebrate milestones.
